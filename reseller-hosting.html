What is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting is where you resell hosting to your own clients. It enables you to create web hosting plans and host your clients (or your own) websites without

The overheads needed to run your own servers. You are provided with an admin panel where you can create web hosting plans, define resources, such as disk space and mail boxes, and manage your hosting account. Your clients, in turn, have their controlpanel allowing them to manage their hosting.
